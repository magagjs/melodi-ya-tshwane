Christian Men's Ministry (CMM)

BINA CMM BINA!!!

Values

The Christian Men’s Ministry of URCSA is founded upon the following values:

  • Faith in the Holy Trinity (Father, Son and the Holy Spirit).
  • Patience and dedication to the service of God.
  • Humility and humbleness, in serving God and one another.
  • Love, kindness and care towards others.
  • Truthfulness, trustworthiness towards one another.
  • Respect for oneself and one another.

Vision

To be a Christian Men’s Ministry that strives to the optimal realization of current and future potential skills in the URCSA Christian Men Community in order to participate actively in the mission of God to the World.

Mission

The mission of the Christian Men’s Ministry is to:

  • Worship, build the faith and the spirituality of men so that they can be faithful witnesses for Christ in the church and in the society, oppose that which is in conflict with the Christian faith both within and outside the Church of Christ.
  • Work in partnership with other Ministries within and with the Church in its ecumenical relations in building a community of believers where the walls of gender, race, language and culture are demolished and equality acknowledged.

Objectives

The objectives of the Christian Men’s Ministry are:

  • To promote harmonization of the Church activities between Christian Men’s Ministry members and other ministries.
  • To promote high standards of professionalism in preaching and practicing the true gospel of Jesus Christ.
  • To make Jesus Christ known to all.
  • To promote stewardship among church members.
  • To promote harmonious holy living between men and their families.
  • To study God’s word and enrich the lives of fellow Christians.
  • To address the unique problems / challenges, needs and aspirations of church community.
